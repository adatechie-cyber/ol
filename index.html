About the Conference

Shaping the Future of
Agricultural Biotechnology

AgriTech-One 2026 is structured around the Strategic Dialogue on the Future of EU Agriculture initiative, bringing together researchers, policymakers, startups, and practitioners from around the world.

Diamond Open Access

100% free to publish and read. No APCs. Global accessibility for researchers from conflict-affected and low-income settings where travel is not possible.

Hybrid Format

Fully remote asynchronous video presentations, interactive digital posters, and live sessions from Zentralbibliothek — TU Berlin. Engage from anywhere.

Double-Anonymized Review

Rigorous double-blind peer review process with open peer review transparency. Every accepted poster receives a unique DOI for immediate citable credit.

Global South Focus

12 scholarships for participants from Global South and low-income countries. A distributed volunteer editorial team of 120 researchers ensures inclusive participation.

AI & Deep Tech

Highlighting AI-based biotechnologies, genome editing, synthetic biology, and low-tech tools for smallholders in unstable regions. Tech-to-Market focus for startups.

FAO & OpenAIRE Repository

Conference proceedings published in the FAO Knowledge Repository and European OpenAIRE. Zenodo repository with Google Scholar indexing and EU Metadata compliance.

Important Dates

Conference Timeline

Mark your calendar for these key milestones leading up to the conference.

25July

Abstract Submission Deadline

Submit your abstract for posters, video presentations, or rapid pitches. Double-anonymized review begins immediately after.

15Aug

Notification of Acceptance

Authors receive peer review feedback and acceptance decisions. DOI assignments and presentation guidelines distributed.

25Sept

Conference Launch

Two-day hybrid event at TU Berlin Zentralbibliothek and fully remote. Opening plenaries and technical sessions begin.

20Oct

Post-Conference Publication

Abstract Book and Conference Proceedings with ISBN and ISSN published. VOD pipeline built. Blockchain-verified certificates issued.
